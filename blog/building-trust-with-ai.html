Building Trust with AI: Keep Customers Comfortable

Best Practices • 4 min read

  1. Be transparent: Let people know when they’re speaking with an AI agent.
  2. Stay consistent: Match your brand voice across chat, email, and phone.
  3. Keep a human fallback: Make it easy to reach a real person anytime.
  4. Show value fast: Have the agent solve real problems early.
  5. Ask for feedback: Review transcripts and refine tone regularly.

AI frees your team to do meaningful work—trust is how you make it stick.
