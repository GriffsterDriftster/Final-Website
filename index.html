Frequently asked questions

What is an AI agent?
Our AI agents are pre‑built automations that combine language models, workflows, and integrations to perform tasks like answering questions, booking meetings, processing invoices, and following up with leads.
How long does it take to get started?
We typically deploy your first agent within two to four weeks, depending on the complexity of your workflows and integrations. A paid pilot is available to prove value quickly.
Do I need technical expertise?
No coding is required. Our team handles configuration and maintenance, and you can iterate on prompts and workflows without retraining.
Can I try before committing?
No, but you can cancel anytime. We’re happy to show you how the system works and give you a live demonstration.
