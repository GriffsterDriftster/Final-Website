Our mission & approach

Onbrd Solutions was founded to help businesses automate the busywork that slows them down. Our calm, capable AI agents let your team focus on what matters most: serving customers and growing your business. We believe AI should be accessible, transparent, and designed with people in mind.

Our process

Discovery

We learn your workflows, systems, and success metrics to identify quick wins and longer‑term opportunities.

Paid Pilot

A low‑commitment engagement where we deploy one agent in 2–4 weeks and prove value against agreed KPIs.

Build

We configure the rest of your agents, integrate with your systems, and set up governance and analytics.

Iterate

Ongoing support to refine prompts, workflows, and models based on feedback and evolving needs.

Our stack

We build on trusted platforms like Zapier, n8n, leading language models, and major CRMs to orchestrate your operations. Our modular architecture means we can plug into the tools you already use.
